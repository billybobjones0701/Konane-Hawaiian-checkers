How to Play Kōnane

Kōnane is a traditional Hawaiian strategy game. The board begins filled with alternating black and white pieces.

Objective

Be the last player able to move. You move by jumping over your opponent's pieces and capturing them.

Gameplay

  1. Removal Phase: Black removes one piece from a center square or corner. White then removes a piece adjacent to the empty space.
  2. Jumping: On your turn, select one of your pieces. It may jump over one or more enemy pieces in a straight line into an empty space. All jumped pieces are captured.
  3. Endgame: If you cannot jump, you lose.

Pass & Play

Two players share the same screen. The board rotates after each move so both can see it properly.

Playing Against AI

Choose a difficulty before starting. The AI will not undo moves.
